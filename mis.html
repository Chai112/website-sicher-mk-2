Maintenance Inspection Services

Sicher engineering experts provide an end-to-end approach to accountability, consistency, and compliance at every stage.

Overview

Sicher offers our engineering expertise to aid in maintenance, inspection, testing and training solutions to assist you with all aspects of the Maintenance and Inspection process.

Our services include installed equipment inspection, factory inspection, Inspection & NDT Services, and Electrical & Instrument Valve Services to achieve accountability, consistency, and compliance with relevant governmental requirements, codes, standards and regulations.

Training

We endeavor to develop a written practice for mechanical integrity and examination requirements. We also train and certify your staff in accordance with international standards such as EN ISO 9712 (superseding EN473), and ISO 9712.

Non Destructive Testing

Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) testing procedures are utilized for components in industrial plant, vessel and pipeline. We utilize the NDT methodology that do not cause damage to components and systems, to ensure plant reliability, safety, and conformance to legislative requirements.

Mechanical, Electrical and Plumbing (MEP)

Ensuring the safety, availability, and cost-effectiveness of building systems via MEP certification enables designers, installers as well as building owners and operators to offer competitive and attractive products in the real estate market. In order to meet the expectations of owners and occupants, the mechanical, electrical, and plumbing (MEP) systems within buildings are required to function at maximum efficiency. Maximizing the performance of MEP systems requires specialized technical and engineering expertise and advanced electrical system testing techniques.
