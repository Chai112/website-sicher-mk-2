1 December, 2022

Sicher won the contract from Charoen Pokphand (CP) Group

Sicher succeeded to secure the global contract to develop the Global Incident Investigation tool and train Incident Investigators of Charoen Pokphand (CP) Group companies in Thailand and China in Q4,2021. Our EHS experts have studied various international EHS principles and philosophies and developed suitable incident investigation and analysis tools that is suitable for CP Group worldwide.

Eventually, our experts trained more than 150 EHS personnel and investigators in Thailand and P.R.China to understand how to use the tool and build up safety cultures among employees and contractors. As a demonstration of our expertise and service quality, we are trusted by CP, one of the world's conglomerate companies.
