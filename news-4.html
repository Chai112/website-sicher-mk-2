20 November, 2022

Sicher developed electronic Incident and EHS Audit Reporting for Thailand

Sicher was awarded from the Department of Industrial Work, under the Ministry of Factory to develop the online Incident and EHS Audit Reporting System. The online Incident and EHS Audit Reporting has been developed successfully and deployed nation wide since early 2022. The user-friendly online reporting system can be used anywhere and anytime in desktop and/or IOS and Android mobile phones. It helps EHS (Environment, Health, and Safety) personnel to report incidents to the Department of Industrial work in digital form and is used as a self-evaluation tool to inspect the occupational safety and process safety management system. The incident and audit results can be presented to the concerned persons and perform preventive and corrective actions to minimize risks to people and factories.
