Sicher is trusted by the Industrial Estate Authority of Thailand (IEAT)

From 1999 up until now, the Sicher Company Limited has won the contract for three consecutive years from the IEAT to provide consulting and training for related factories. Each year our PSM expert team demonstrates their knowledge and experience and gains a reputation as the leading Process Safety Management in Thailand.

Process safety management (PSM) is a disciplined framework for managing the integrity of operating systems and processes handling hazardous substances by applying good design principles, engineering, and operating practices. PSM principles are applied at facilities that manufacture, store, handle or use highly hazardous chemicals (HHCs).

In Thailand, the Industrial Estate Authority of Thailand (IEAT) has regulated PSM in 2016 (in conformance with the regulations imposed by the Board of Directors of the Industrial Estate Authority of Thailand Governing Rules, Procedures and Conditions for Business Operations in Industrial Estates (No. 4) B.E. 2559) in industries located in the industrial estates that have HHCs, flammable gases, liquids or the mixture of gas and liquid.
