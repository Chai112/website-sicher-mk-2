Overview

Sicher leverages its expansive network of global partners including ADEN group, to lower your facility’s energy footprint, benefitting our environment. We create comprehensive solutions for energy management, on-site renewable energy optimizations, and environmental services to cut carbon and improve efficiency.

We believe that clean and efficient energy should be available to every business that wants to make a difference. Our energy solutions are designed to be turn-key, meaning that we handle the whole project from design to operation. We can even finance the entire project with our Energy as a Service contract for clients looking to transform energy use.

In our services, we improve your existing energy-using assets such as chillers, boilers, air compressors, and HVAC or upgrade to a totally new, highly efficient energy station. All energy solutions can digitally link with our AI+IOT platform, giving your unprecedented control and transparency into your energy use.

Depending on your location, We can design, install and operate the wind turbines and solar panels on-site. With on-site distributed renewable energy, your facility is free from the fossil-fuel-powered grid and can take more control over how and what kind of energy gets used.

In addition, the energy generated by solar panels can be stored for use on days without sun or wind. The AI platform uses an algorithm to find the ideal balance between power use from on-site sources or the grid, maximizing your savings and environmental impact.
