Overview

Sicher’s Environment, Health, and Safety (EHS) software is a cloud-based software that supports companies in automating EHS management and regulatory compliance process and extending these processes to external partners. Our software makes it easy to track and report your safety data, analyze trends and draw insights, meet compliance requirements, and cut down administration work.

Incorporate with Intelex located in Toronto and with our 30 years of experience in EHS make us the market leader for recording, tracking, investigating, managing, and preventing EHS incidents.
