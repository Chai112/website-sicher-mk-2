Construction Safety Management Consulting

Sicher supports our clients by overseeing the daily activities that are performed around construction projects.

Overview

There are many hazards for workers and those nearby construction sites. With over 30 years of knowledge and experience, Sicher supports our clients in overseeing daily activities around construction projects. With more social and governmental stringency on job sites, our EHS experts work with your company to provide close support during safety project planning, daily safety meetings and audits of programs and activities. Furthermore, we incorporate with construction managers and subcontractors to ensure safety within the workplace.

Setup EHS Management System

We work with construction professionals to establish policies for better health and safety practices. We educate managers and encourage them to set the standard for their employees.

Job Safety Analysis (JSA)

Before starting tasks, the workers shall break the task down into steps, identify the associated hazards, and determine corrective actions to eliminate or protect themselves from the identified risks. Eventually, our construction safety consultants improve the JSA process by evaluating and training individuals and workers.

Operating Procedures

We develop EHS operating procedures for your construction projects. We help guide your employees to recognize and apply proper techniques and guidelines for completing a job. The operating systems are covered all Hot-work and Non-Routine Work Permits, Log-out Tag-out, Covid-19 prevention, and many more.

On-Site/Online EHS Safety Training

Our consultants will provide On-site and online EHS training to aid your business in achieving a reduction in incident rates and higher standards of EHS in the workplace. The EHS training includes various topics such as Managerial and Supervisory safety training, Site Specific Rules & Procedures, Working at Height, Confined Space Entry Permits, and Portable firefighting.

Observation Reports

Construction Safety Observation Reports are used as an informal way to conduct job site observations to identify the negative and positive acts or conditions related to construction site safety. Completing these forms is typically the responsibility of all front-line leadership and employees involved with the project. We identify safety trends and evaluate the strong areas of the client as well as areas which increased job site safety focus is required to improve safety standards.
