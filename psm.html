Overview

Process Safety Management (PSM) is a vital tool used in the safety field to provide a systematic approach in preventing catastrophic releases of highly hazardous substances. PSM is a standard set forth by the Industrial Estate Authority of Thailand (IEAT), which the United States of America’s Occupational Safety & Health Administration (OSHA) has adopted since 2017. By preventing release of substances, it will benefit the environment and reduce employee’s exposure to severe health hazards.

PSM Auditing

Industrial Estate Authority of Thailand (IEAT) requires PSM compliance audits are performed every three years. A PSM compliance audit helps companies determine whether they are in compliance with PSM laws. Sicher has been qualified and registered officially: License number 09. Our experienced PSM auditors can support companies to determine whether they are following their PSM policies and protocols.
